What We Do

Areas of Practice

Civil Litigation

Representation in civil disputes involving contracts, property rights, recovery claims, injunctions, and other court proceedings.

Property & real estate law

Legal assistance in property transactions, title disputes, land matters, development agreements, and real estate litigation.

Arbitration & ADR

Alternative dispute resolution (ADR) refers to methods of resolving disputes without resorting to traditional litigation. ADR encompasses processes such as arbitration, mediation, conciliation, negotiation, and other techniques that allow parties to resolve conflicts efficiently

White-Collar Defense & police law interface

Defense in economic offences, fraud investigations, regulatory proceedings, and interactions with law enforcement agencies.

Maintenance & family law adjacent matters

Legal support in maintenance claims, matrimonial disputes, custody matters, and related family law proceedings.

Criminal Litigation & Cyber Crime

Comprehensive legal representation in criminal cases, cybercrime investigations, digital fraud, bail matters, trials, and appeals.

NDPS — Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act

Legal defense and advisory services in cases under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act.

EOW-Economic Offences Wing

ED-Enforcement Directorate

Legal assistance in Economic Offences Wing investigations involving financial fraud, cheating, and corporate misconduct.
Representation before the Enforcement Directorate in financial crime, foreign exchange, and money laundering proceedings.

PMLA-Prevention of Money Laundering Act

Representation in money laundering investigations, attachment proceedings, and matters under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act.

CBI — Central Bureau of Investigation

The CBI is India’s top central investigation agency. Handles: Corruption cases, Major scams, High-profile murders, Economic offences, Cybercrime, Special court-directed investigations

CID — Crime Investigation Department

CID is a specialized branch of the state police. Handles: Serious crimes, Murder investigations, Riots, Fraud, Special criminal intelligence

MCOCA — Maharashtra Control of Organised Crime Act and all types of criminal matters

A very strict law mainly used in Maharashtra against: Organized crime gangs, Mafia, Extortion rackets, Underworld activities
Example: If a gang repeatedly commits serious crimes for illegal profit, MCOCA may be applied.

RERA — Real Estate Regulatory Authority

RERA was created to protect: Home buyers, Property investors, Flat purchasers
from fraud and delays by builders/developers.

Banking, Finance & Recovery Matters

These are disputes related to: Loans disputes, Banks , EMI defaults, Recovery notices, Financial fraud, Cheque bounce, Debt recovery

Dispute Resolution

Solving legal or commercial conflicts between parties. Instead of only going through long court trials, disputes may be resolved through different mechanisms.
